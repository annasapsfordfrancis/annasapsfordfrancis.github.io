Soul-Eater

A dark fantasy novel.

Elska is turning into a monster…

Elska should have died in the forest. That’s how the story was meant to go. She was to be the first victim of a creature of ancient and terrible evil: a soul-eater. Her fate was to die and be forgotten.

Sometimes the story has to change.

As an archon priest, Isren was taught that soul-eaters don’t leave survivors—only lifeless husks and more of their own kind. So when he rescues Elska only moments too late from a soul-eater attack he knows where his duty lies. She must be executed. Unfortunately, things don’t quite go to plan.

Now the soul-eater is back to finish what it started. And Elska will learn: it takes a monster to fight a monster.
